Information We Collect

1. Location Data

We collect your device's location to:

  • Determine Qibla direction

Important: Location data is processed locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers. We do not store or share your precise location.

2. Device Information

We may collect:

This information helps us improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Advertising Data

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable identifier for advertising purposes)
  • General location (country/region level, not precise)
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

4. Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Prayer tracking history
  • Athkar and tasbih counts
  • Fitness and workout progress
  • Water and food tracking data
  • Personal health metrics (height, weight, age)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Saved duas and favorites

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export a backup file.

We use the collected information to:
